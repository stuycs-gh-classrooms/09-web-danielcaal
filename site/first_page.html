About Us

We are a cross-denominational fellowship of Christians and those curious about the Christian Faith. We hold meetings every Tuesday and Friday. Our central meetings on Tuesdays are usually held in 335. We play games, have conversations, sing songs, and study the Bible together. Sometimes, we will have food or a special guest for special occasions or holidays. On Fridays, we host prayer meetings.
